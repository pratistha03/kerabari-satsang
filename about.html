About Our Sacred Journey

Discover the divine heritage and spiritual mission of our satsang community

The Sacred History of Kerabari Satsang

In the blessed village of Kerabari, nestled in the heart of West Bengal, our spiritual community was born from the divine grace of Sri Sri Thakur Anukulchandra Chakravarty. For over seven decades, this sacred land has been a beacon of spiritual light, drawing devotees from across the world.

The satsang tradition in Kerabari began as a humble gathering of sincere seekers who were touched by the profound teachings of Sri Sri Thakur. What started as intimate spiritual discussions has blossomed into a vibrant community dedicated to the pursuit of truth, love, and divine realization.

The Foundation of Faith

"Where there is sincere devotion and collective aspiration for the Divine, there the presence of the Guru manifests most powerfully. Kerabari stands as a testament to this eternal truth, where countless souls have found their spiritual home."

Our Spiritual Journey Through Time

1950s

The Sacred Beginning

The first satsang gatherings began in Kerabari, with devoted followers coming together to study and practice the teachings of Sri Sri Thakur Anukulchandra.

1970s

Community Growth

The satsang community expanded significantly, establishing regular prayer meetings, spiritual discourses, and community service programs.

1990s

Temple Construction

The main temple complex was built, providing a permanent sacred space for worship, meditation, and community gatherings.

2000s

Digital Outreach

Embracing modern technology to spread Sri Sri Thakur's teachings globally through online satsangs and digital publications.

Present

Global Community

Today, our satsang serves thousands of devotees worldwide, maintaining the pure essence of Sri Sri Thakur's teachings while adapting to contemporary needs.

Our Mission & Vision

Spiritual Awakening

To guide souls on their journey toward divine realization through the timeless teachings of Sri Sri Thakur, fostering inner peace and spiritual growth.

Selfless Service

To serve humanity with love and compassion, following the principle that service to mankind is service to the Divine.

Universal Brotherhood

To create a harmonious world where all beings live in unity, transcending barriers of caste, creed, and nationality.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a world transformed by the divine love and wisdom of Sri Sri Thakur, where every individual recognizes their inherent divinity and lives in harmony with all creation. Through our satsang, we strive to be instruments of this divine transformation, spreading light, love, and spiritual consciousness to every corner of the earth.

The Eternal Teachings of Sri Sri Thakur

Core Principles

  • Anusaran (Following): Complete surrender and devotion to the Divine Guru
  • Prema (Divine Love): Cultivating unconditional love for all beings
  • Seva (Service): Selfless service as a path to spiritual purification
  • Sadhana (Practice): Regular spiritual practice and discipline
  • Satsang (Holy Company): Association with like-minded spiritual seekers

Sri Sri Thakur Anukulchandra Chakravarty's teachings emphasize the importance of living a life centered on divine love and service. His philosophy transcends religious boundaries, offering a universal path to spiritual realization that is accessible to all sincere seekers, regardless of their background or beliefs.

Through his profound wisdom, Sri Sri Thakur has shown us that the ultimate purpose of human existence is to realize our divine nature and to serve as instruments of God's love in the world. This realization comes not through mere intellectual understanding, but through sincere practice, devotion, and the grace of the Guru.

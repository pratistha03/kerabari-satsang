The Eternal Light of Satsang

In the sacred village of Kerabari, we gather in the spirit of satsang to honor the divine teachings of Sri Sri Thakur Anukulchandra Chakravarty. Our community is dedicated to spreading love, compassion, and spiritual wisdom through collective prayer, meditation, and service to humanity.

Through the grace of Sri Sri Thakur, we strive to create a harmonious world where every soul can find peace, purpose, and divine connection. Join us in this sacred journey of spiritual awakening and selfless service.

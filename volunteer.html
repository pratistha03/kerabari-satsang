Volunteer Guidelines

Spiritual Commitment

  • Maintain a devotional attitude in all service activities
  • Respect the sacred nature of our temple and community
  • Follow the teachings and principles of Sri Sri Thakur
  • Participate in regular satsangs when possible

Community Harmony

  • Treat all community members with love and respect
  • Maintain unity and avoid divisive discussions
  • Support fellow volunteers and work as a team
  • Embrace diversity and welcome all sincere seekers

Responsibilities

  • Be punctual and reliable in your commitments
  • Communicate any schedule changes in advance
  • Complete assigned tasks with dedication and care
  • Maintain confidentiality when appropriate

Personal Conduct

  • Dress modestly and appropriately for temple service
  • Maintain personal hygiene and cleanliness
  • Abstain from alcohol, tobacco, and non-vegetarian food during service
  • Practice patience, humility, and selfless service

The Spirit of Service

"True service is not about what we do, but about the love and devotion with which we do it. When we serve others, we serve the Divine within them, and in doing so, we purify our own hearts and draw closer to the ultimate truth."

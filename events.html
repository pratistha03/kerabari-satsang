Sacred Memories - Past Events

Diwali Celebration 2023

November 12, 2023

Our grand Diwali celebration brought together over 500 devotees for a night of divine light, cultural performances, and spiritual joy. The temple was beautifully decorated with thousands of diyas, creating an atmosphere of divine radiance. Special prayers were offered for world peace and prosperity.

Guru Purnima 2023

July 3, 2023

A deeply moving Guru Purnima celebration honoring Sri Sri Thakur Anukulchandra. Devotees from across the region gathered to express gratitude to the divine Guru through special pujas, devotional songs, and sharing of spiritual experiences. The day concluded with a community feast and distribution of prasadam.

Summer Spiritual Retreat 2023

May 15-17, 2023

A transformative 3-day retreat that provided participants with deep spiritual insights and inner peace. The retreat included guided meditation sessions, spiritual discourses, nature walks, and personal reflection time. Many participants reported profound spiritual experiences and renewed dedication to their spiritual practice.

Annual Charity Drive 2023

March 20, 2023

Our annual charity drive successfully served over 1,000 families in need. Volunteers distributed food packages, clothing, and educational materials to underprivileged communities. The event embodied Sri Sri Thakur's teaching that service to humanity is service to the Divine, bringing joy and relief to many families.
